Hatchbacks

Description:

atchbacks are small to mid-sized cars with a rear door that swings upward, offering easy access to the cargo area. They’re often compact but spacious, making them great for urban driving.


Examples:

Volkswagen Golf, Ford Focus, Honda Civic Hatchback


New Price Range:

$20,000 - $35,000

Coupes

Description:

Coupes are two-door cars often built with a sporty design. They’re usually smaller than sedans and are favored for style and performance rather than passenger space.


Examples:

BMW 4 Series, Audi A5, Chevrolet Camaro


New Price Range:

$30,000 - $70,000

Convertibles

Description:

Convertibles feature a retractable roof, which can be either soft or hard-top, offering an open-air driving experience. They are often similar to coupes in style but provide the option for roof-down driving.


Examples:

Mazda MX-5 Miata, Ford Mustang Convertible, BMW Z4


New Price Range:

$30,000 - $80,000

SUVs (Sport Utility Vehicles)

Description:

SUVs come in various sizes (compact, mid-size, and full-size) and are known for their higher seating position and spacious interior. They often offer all-wheel or four-wheel drive and are popular with families.


Examples:

Toyota RAV4 (compact), Ford Explorer (mid-size), Chevrolet Tahoe (full-size)


New Price Range:

$25,000 - $80,000+

Crossovers (CUVs)

Description:

Crossovers are similar to SUVs but built on a car platform (often with unibody construction). They are popular for their car-like handling and higher ground clearance.


Examples:

Honda CR-V, Mazda CX-5, Subaru Forester


New Price Range:

$25,000 - $40,000

Pickup Trucks

Description:

Known for their utility and towing capacity, pickup trucks feature an open cargo bed. They are available in various sizes and are highly popular in North America, especially for work and outdoor use.


Examples:

Ford F-150, Chevrolet Silverado, RAM 1500


New Price Range:

$30,000 - $80,000+

Minivans

Description:

Minivans are spacious vehicles built for passenger comfort and storage, making them ideal for large families. They offer sliding doors and versatile seating.


Examples:

Honda Odyssey, Toyota Sienna, Chrysler Pacifica


New Price Range:

$35,000 - $50,000

Luxury Cars

Description:

Luxury cars provide premium features, high-quality materials, and superior comfort. They range from sedans to SUVs and offer advanced technology and powerful performance.


Examples:

Mercedes-Benz E-Class, BMW 7 Series, Audi Q7


New Price Range:

$50,000 - $150,000+

Sports Cars

Description:

Built for performance and speed, sports cars have powerful engines, agile handling, and a sleek design. They’re often more expensive and designed for an exhilarating driving experience.


Examples:

Porsche 911, Chevrolet Corvette, Jaguar F-Type


New Price Range:

$60,000 - $200,000+

Electric Vehicles (EVs)

Description:

Electric vehicles run entirely on electric power, producing zero emissions. They range from affordable compact cars to luxury and performance models.


Examples:

Tesla Model 3, Nissan Leaf, Ford Mustang Mach-E


New Price Range:

$30,000 - $100,000+

Hybrid Vehicles

xDescription:

Hybrids combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor for improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. They’re often available in standard sedan and SUV formats.


Examples:

Toyota Prius, Honda Accord Hybrid, Ford Escape Hybrid


New Price Range:

$25,000 - $45,000

Luxury SUVs

Description:

Luxury SUVs combine the spaciousness and versatility of an SUV with the premium features, comfort, and materials of luxury cars. Many have advanced technology and powerful engines.


Examples:

Range Rover, Mercedes-Benz G-Class, Lexus LX


New Price Range:

$70,000 - $200,000+

Off-Road Vehicles

Description:

Designed for rugged terrain, off-road vehicles are equipped with features like all-terrain tires, advanced suspension, and high ground clearance. They are popular for outdoor and adventure driving.


Examples:

Jeep Wrangler, Toyota Land Cruiser, Ford Bronco


New Price Range:

$30,000 - $80,000+

Station Wagons

Description:

Station wagons combine the car-like handling of sedans with the cargo capacity of an SUV. They’re designed for practicality with ample space and are often associated with European carmakers.


Examples:

Volvo V60, Subaru Outback, Audi A4 Allroad


New Price Range:

$35,000 - $70,000
